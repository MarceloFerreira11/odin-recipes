Bacalhau à Brás

Bacalhau à Brás served in a classical style

This cod recipe, with eggs and potatoes, is one of the most popular among the Portuguese. At the same time, it is also one of the easiest and quickest to make. You can easily adapt the method and try it with other fish or shredded chicken. It always looks good

Ingredients

  • Fresh Parsley
  • Potatos
  • Cod
  • Oil
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Black Olives
  • Onions

    • Steps

    1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin sticks (if you are using regular potatoes)(You can use "palha" friend potatoes)
    2. Remove skin and bones from the cod and shred it
    3. Fry the potatoes you peeled in step1
    4. Sauté the chopped onion with garlic and a bay leaf
    5. When the onion and garlic starts to become soft, add the shredded cod and cover the pan
    6. cook over low heat until the cod turns white
    7. remove the bay leaf from the mixture and add the fries
    8. Add the eggs, previously beaten and stir them gently, allowing them to coat all the ingredients. DONT LET THEM COOK AND DRY OUT TOO MUCH!
    9. Before serving, sprinkle with chopped parsly and decore with Olives
