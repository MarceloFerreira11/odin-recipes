Bacalhau à Brás

Bacalhau à Brás served in a classical style

This cod recipe, with eggs and potatoes, is one of the most popular among the Portuguese. At the same time, it is also one of the easiest and quickest to make. You can easily adapt the method and try it with other fish or shredded chicken. It always looks good

Ingredients

  • Fresh Parsley
  • Potatos
  • Cod
  • Oil
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Black Olives
  • Onions
